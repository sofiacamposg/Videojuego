CAT CAFE

What is a cat cafe?

A cat café is a themed establishment where customers can enjoy drinks and food while socializing, petting and interacting with cats that roam freely around the place.

Example of a cat cafe

Where do cat cafes come from?

Originally from Taiwan and popularized in Japan, these spaces usually shelter rescued cats and promote responsible adoption, offering a relaxed environment for cat lovers.

Key features of cat cafes:

🐾 Feline interaction

The cats are the stars, roaming through adapted areas with scratching posts and toys.

🐾 Adoption and rescue

Many work as temporary shelters where you can meet cats available for adoption.

🐾 House rules

Guests must follow rules such as not waking the cats, not feeding them, and sometimes paying an entry fee or time-based reservation.

🐾 Themed experience

Both the food (breads, crepes) and the decoration usually feature cat motifs.

🐾 Main goal

To offer relaxation through feline therapy and raise awareness about animal care.
