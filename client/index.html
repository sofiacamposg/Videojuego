STORY

Long ago, in the heart of the Roman Empire, the Colosseum stood as a symbol of power, blood, and glory. Warriors from distant lands were captured, trained, and forced to fight for the entertainment of the masses.

You are one of them.

A gladiator thrown into the arena with nothing but your weapon and your will to survive. Each battle brings new dangers, stronger enemies, and the unpredictable help of the crowd.

The Emperor watches. The people demand spectacle.

Will you rise as a legend… or fall forgotten in the sand?

1 / 10

Objective

Defeat the required number of enemies to complete each level. Survive all three levels to win the run. If you lose your life, the game is over and you restart from Level 1.

2 / 10

Controls

A / ← : Move left

D / → : Move right

Space : Jump

J : Attack (in the direction you are facing)

C : Open your card deck

Esc : Pause

3 / 10

Archetypes

Choose one at the start of each run. Your choice affects your HP, damage, and speed.

Warrior — Balanced | sword and shield. HP: 120, Attack: 20, Speed: 5.

Lancer — Agile | spear. HP: 100, Attack: 25, Speed: 6.

Heavy — Tank | full armor. HP: 150, Attack: 15, Speed: 3.

4 / 10

Lives

You start each run with 1 life. If you die, it's Game Over and you restart from Level 1.

You can buy more lives or Galen's remedy in the store, but only famous gladiators have this privilege.

Galen's Remedy will aid you when you stand between life and death, but it can only be used once per match.

5 / 10

Fame

Fame increases based on how quickly you complete each level compared to the target time. Higher fame helps you get upgrades and affects the cards you receive.

6 / 10

Cards

Two card events occur during each level:

Event 1 — end of level: The emperor rewards your effort. If you are famous, he'll give you 2 power-ups, but if not, he'll give you one. You better give your best!
You can use these cards any time in the level, only press 'C' to know which ones you have.

Event 2 — mid-level: The crowd wants to help you, but you aren't everyone's favorite. Three cards appear face down, there could be punishments or power-ups, let your fate choose.
Their effect will apply immediately.

7 / 10

Power-ups

Favor of the People: +20% movement speed for the level duration.

Blade of Mars: +30% attack damage for the level duration.

Blessing of Venus: instantly recover 1 heart of health.

Lion's Roar: all current enemies are slowed for 5 seconds.

Divine Shield: absorbs the next hit with no damage taken.

Gladiator's Blood: each enemy kill restores a small amount of HP for the level duration.

Colosseum's Fury: basic attack gains area of effect for 8 seconds.

Eye of the Emperor: reveals the type of the next 3 cards before selection.

8 / 10

Punishments

Imperial Decree: 2 additional enemies spawn immediately.

Chains of Caesar: jumping is disabled for 10 seconds.

Hunger of the Plebs: using any card costs half a heart of health.

Wrath of Jupiter: instantly lose 1 heart of health.

Amphitheatre Fog: the screen is partially darkened for 12 seconds.

Lanista's Betrayal: your attack damage is reduced by 40% for 15 seconds.

Senate's Judgment: if you die during this level, 2 lives are lost instead of 1.

9 / 10

Levels

Level 1: You have to hunt lions. No floor hazards, but it won't be that easy, lions are the strongest.

Level 2: You fight against tigers. Spikes appear in random floor positions. Both can kill you, watch out!

Level 3: Your enemies are boars. Spikes and fire zones appear in random floor positions. You can die in any second, losing all your progress.

10 / 10

Hazards

Spikes (levels 2 and 3): deal fixed damage per second while you stand on them. Move away to stop taking damage.

Fire (level 3 only): deal 40% of your maximum HP per second. Get out of the zone immediately.
