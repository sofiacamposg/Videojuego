STORY

Long ago, in the heart of the Roman Empire, the Colosseum stood as a symbol of power, blood, and glory. Warriors from distant lands were captured, trained, and forced to fight for the entertainment of the masses.

You are one of them.

A gladiator thrown into the arena with nothing but your weapon and your will to survive. Each battle brings new dangers, stronger enemies, and the unpredictable favor of the crowd.

The Emperor watches. The people demand spectacle.

Will you rise as a legend… or fall forgotten in the sand?

INSTRUCTIONS

OBJECTIVE

Defeat the required number of enemies to complete each level. Survive long enough and prove your strength in the arena.

CONTROLS

← : Move Left

→ : Move Right

Space : Jump

J : Attack

GAMEPLAY

Move across obstacles, avoid falling, and fight enemies. Each level becomes more dangerous as stronger enemies appear.

During the match, you will be offered cards that can either help you or punish you. Choose wisely, every decision affects your survival.

IMPORTANT

If you die, you lose a life and restart the level. If you lose all your lives, the game is over and you restart from Level 1.
